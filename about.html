ABOUT

Run out of his parents study, eyre is an independent design studio owned by Torquay based illustrator and designer, Tristan Stephens. Established in 2016, eyre has been committed to creating chic designs both digitally and manually through a diverse range of mediums. Currently studying Graphic Design and Multimedia, Tristan continues to expand his knowledge and expertise
